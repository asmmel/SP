TRANSFORMATION PATHWAY: PREMIUM FORMULATIONS

TRANSFORMATION PATHWAY

EMERALD RESTORATION

A deeply restorative blend designed to optimize metabolic function and enhance natural detoxification pathways.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup filtered glacier water
  • 1 cup organic baby spinach
  • ½ organic avocado
  • ½ organic cucumber
  • 1 tablespoon cold-pressed MCT oil
  • 1 tablespoon organic hemp seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon Himalayan sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • Juice of ½ organic lemon
  • 3 ice cubes (filtered water)

Method: Blend on high power for 45 seconds until silky smooth consistency is achieved.

RUBY METABOLIC ACTIVATOR

Engineered to stimulate thermogenesis and enhance metabolic efficiency throughout the day.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup organic mixed berries (blackberry, raspberry, blueberry)
  • 1 tablespoon raw almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ceylon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon raw cacao nibs
  • 1 scoop high-quality collagen peptides (optional)
  • 5 fresh mint leaves
  • Stevia extract to taste (optional)

Method: Blend on high power for 60 seconds to ensure optimal texture and nutrient extraction.

VITALITY PATHWAY: PREMIUM FORMULATIONS

VITALITY PATHWAY

GOLDEN COGNITIVE ENHANCER

A precision-crafted blend designed to optimize mental clarity, focus, and sustained cognitive performance.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup ceremonial-grade coconut water
  • 1 tablespoon cold-pressed olive oil
  • ½ cup organic mango chunks
  • ¼ cup organic pineapple
  • 1 tablespoon raw walnuts
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric root (freshly grated)
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ inch fresh ginger (peeled)
  • 1 teaspoon bee pollen
  • 1 teaspoon lion's mane mushroom powder
  • Small handful of baby spinach

Method: Blend at high velocity for 50 seconds to ensure optimal emulsification of oils and complete extraction of bioactive compounds.

AZURE PERFORMANCE ELIXIR

Formulated to enhance cellular energy production and optimize physical performance through strategic nutrient timing.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • ½ frozen banana
  • ½ cup organic blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 teaspoon maca powder
  • 1 teaspoon spirulina
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla bean powder
  • 1 teaspoon cordyceps mushroom powder
  • Small handful of kale
  • 1 Medjool date (optional)

Method: Blend on progressive power, starting low and increasing to maximum for 60 seconds total blending time.

RENEWAL PATHWAY: PREMIUM FORMULATIONS

RENEWAL PATHWAY

AMETHYST CELLULAR RENEWAL

A sophisticated blend designed to activate cellular detoxification pathways and support systemic renewal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup filtered spring water
  • 1 cup organic red cabbage
  • ½ cup frozen organic blueberries
  • ½ cup frozen blackberries
  • 1 tablespoon cold-pressed flaxseed oil
  • 1 tablespoon activated charcoal
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseeds
  • 1 teaspoon milk thistle extract
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • Small piece fresh ginger

Method: Blend at ascending speeds, beginning gently and progressing to maximum power for a total of 70 seconds.

JADE REJUVENATION COMPLEX

Meticulously formulated to support liver function and enhance the body's natural elimination processes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup coconut water
  • 1 cup organic dandelion greens
  • ½ cup organic cucumber
  • ½ green apple
  • 1 stalk organic celery
  • 1 tablespoon cold-pressed extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon hemp seeds
  • 1 teaspoon chlorella powder
  • ½ inch fresh ginger root
  • Small handful fresh cilantro
  • Pinch of Himalayan salt

Method: Blend progressively for 60 seconds, ensuring complete integration of ingredients without excessive oxidation.
